Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 19, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Leads to Sheets collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our Facebook Lead Ads to Google Sheets integration service.

1. Information We Collect

Our service integrates data from Facebook Lead Ads into Google Sheets. We collect and process the following information:

  • Lead information submitted through Facebook Lead Ads, which may include names, email addresses, phone numbers, and any other information provided in the lead form.
  • Facebook Page ID and Form ID associated with the lead.
  • Timestamps of when leads are generated and processed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To transfer lead data from Facebook to the specified Google Sheet.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our integration service.
  • To respond to your requests or questions about our service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Lead data is transferred directly from Facebook to Google Sheets. We do not store this data on our servers. However, the data will be stored in your Google Sheet according to your Google account's settings and privacy policies.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share any information collected through our service with third parties, except as described in this policy or as required by law.

5. Your Rights and Choices

As our service transfers data directly to your Google Sheet, you have full control over this data. You can view, modify, or delete this data directly in your Google Sheet. For information submitted through Facebook Lead Ads, please refer to Facebook's data policies for guidance on managing that data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

Our legal basis for collecting and using the personal information described in this Privacy Policy depends on the personal information we collect and the specific context in which we collect it. We process your personal information based on your consent given through Facebook Lead Ads and our legitimate interests in providing and improving our integration service.

9. Data Retention

We retain the processed information in your Google Sheet until you choose to delete it. We recommend reviewing and managing your data in Google Sheets regularly.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the country you live in. These countries may have data protection laws different from your country. By using our service, you consent to your information being transferred to and processed in the United States and any other country where Google or Facebook operates.
